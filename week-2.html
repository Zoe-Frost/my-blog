Yesterday I brainstormed ideas for the type brief. Today I will be finalising my problem statement and pinpointing the specific issue in the fast fashion industry more clearly. No blockers today.

Tuesday

Yesterday I finished pinpointing a specific problem and creating my problem statement, I also ideated new solutions that I could use in my brand. Today I will be finalising my ideas for interviews and starting on my interview plan. No blockers at the moment.

Week 2 reflection

This week I got a good lot of things done and the start of my interviews went really well. My documentation on the other hand was much slower due to computer difficulties. I need to be taking more breaks so that I don't get stuck on certain ideas and instead flow better in my work process. For the next sprint I will be working in intervals of 30 minutes and then taking 5-10 minute breaks.
