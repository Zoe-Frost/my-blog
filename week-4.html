Week 4

Here are the reflections from my fourth week in term two of BMD2ID.

Monday

Yesterday I tested my paper prototype and typed in the insights I gained from that as well as changes that I could make. I also thought about what I need to consider when it comes to the shipping method and how it will work. Today I will be doing my lo-fi prototype and testing it with the wireframes. I am having a few blockers when it comes to changing the layout.

Tuesday

Yesterday I started on my lo-fi prototype and thought about who I will be testing it with in my audience as well as booked in meetings with them to test it. Today I will be finishing my lo-fi prototype and testing it. No blockers.

Wednesday

Yesterday I finished my lo-fi prototype and tested it with my audience. I looked through the insight and wrote in the main ones and the changes I should make. Today I spent putting together my pitch presentation and practicing how I will pitch it. The blocker I had today was how I want my tone of voice to sound when presenting.

Thursday

Yesterday I put together my presentation and started on my design strategy. Today I will be finishing my design strategy as well as practicing to pitch tomorrow. No blockers.

Friday

Yesterday I finished my design strategy and practiced how I was going to pitch. Today I will be pitching my idea and presenting it to my peers. No blockers.

Week 4 reflection

This week I had a good time getting through my work. I found that my tests helped me to get a clearer idea of how I want my website to look and feel. I felt that I did reasonably well in my pitch presentation which I am happy with. For what didn't go so well, I feel that I need to become more decisive as well as being more confident in my ideas, when I am pitching I sound uncertain to the audience which makes them feel less confident about the idea regardless if it's good or not. In the future, I need to be proud of what I am doing and speak with certainty to sell the ideas as opposed to questioning it.
